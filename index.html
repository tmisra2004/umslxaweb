UMSL Chi Alpha

Thank you for your interest in Chi Alpha at the University of Missouri-St. Louis! We are a movement of college-age men and women earnestly following Jesus.
Our mission is to reconcile students to Christ, equipping them through spirit-filled communities of prayer, worship, fellowship, discipleship, and mission to transform the university, the marketplace and the world.
Although we are a Christian group, many of our friends are not Christians and come from various parts of the world, including the U.S.A!
We welcome people from all different backgrounds and faiths to participate in our activities.
Chi Alpha Christian Fellowship has Greek letters in its name, but we are not a fraternity, we are a family!
